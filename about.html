About the Company

A top-notch, technology-oriented supply company incorporated to serve Nigeria's military, paramilitary and corporate institutions.

Who We Are

Bridging the gaps in military logistics

FAVDAVCON AND CG NIG LTD was incorporated on 2nd October 2024 under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 (RC 7971283). Our services range from military and paramilitary procurement of accoutrements, such as uniform materials and kits, to office furniture, operational vehicles, printing services, environmental services such as beautification and fumigation, and security and corporate services.

No army in the world succeeds without logistics. Our purpose is to complement the Nigerian Armed Forces and paramilitary organisations with the supply of quality accoutrements for the adequate and smart functioning of the system.

Our Vision

To supply military accoutrement, office equipment and all other accessories that enable the military to perform their roles effectively and efficiently.

Our Mission

To ensure our military and paramilitary function adequately for the sustenance of peace and security in Nigeria.
